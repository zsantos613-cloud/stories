The Ichthyosaurus is a great example of convergent evolution, where species of different lineages develop similar features. While the Ichthyosaurus looks like a dolphin, it's actually a marine reptile sharing a streamlined body and dorsal fins with the mammal. This body allowed it to quickly hunt within the Mesozoic oceans.
