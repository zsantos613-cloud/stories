Irritator challengeri

IRRITATOR

PISCIVORE🐟

PERIOD

Early Cretaceous

FAMILY

Spinosauridae

REGION

South America

SOURCE

Jurassic World Evolution 3, Wetlands Pack

A fish-eating theropod dinosaur adapted to hunting prey in aquatic environments related to Spinosaurus. It's name (literally meaning what it is) came from the fact that the paleontologists felt 'irritated' by the fake reconstruction of its skull which was altered by fossil poachers. The name reflects the frustration of the people restoring the tampered skull.
