Tyrannosaurus Rex

"Tyrant Lizard King"

Time: Cretaceous

Diet: Carnivorous

Family: Tyrannosauridae

Region: North America

Source: Prehistoric Kingdom




The Tyrannosaurus Rex was one of the heaviest terrestrial predators to ever live and is popularly known as 'The King of the Dinosaurs'. Found in the Morrison formation alongside Ankylosaurus and Triceratops, the Tyrannosaurus possessed one of the most powerful bite forces in order to take down such prey establishing itself as the ecosystem's Apex Predator!

The Tyrannosaurus (T-Rex) was one of the heaviest terrestrial predators to ever live. It roamed North America during the Late Cretaceous (~66–68 million years ago), possessing one of the most powerful bite forces in the animal kingdom.
