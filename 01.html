Tyrannosaurus Rex

"Tyrant Lizard King"

Time:Cretaceous

Diet:Carnivorous

Family:Tyrannosauridae

Region:North America

Source:Prehistoric Kingdom

The Tyrannosaurus (also known as T-Rex) was one of the heaviest terrestrial predators to ever live. It roamed North America during the Late Cretaceous Period around 66-68 millions of years ago living the Hell Creek Formation alongside Triceratops and Ankylosaurus. Popularly known as 'the king of dinosaurs', it possessed one of the most powerful bite forces in the animal kingdom in order to easily hunt prey in its ecosystem.
