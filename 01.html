Prehistoric Kingdom

The Tyrannosaurus Rex was one of the heaviest terrestrial predators to ever live and is popularly known as "The King of the Dinosaurs." Found in the Hell Creek Formation alongside Ankylosaurus and Triceratops, it possessed one of the most powerful bite forces, allowing it to dominate its ecosystem as an apex predator.
