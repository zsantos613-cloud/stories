Let's get one thing straight, the Velociraptor was NOT a tiger-sized featherless dinosaur that could open kitchen doors. But rather a small yet cunning predator adorned with feathers that pursued its prey with speed using its iconic enlarged talon to latch onto its prey. While its not as big or murderous as it was presented within popular movies, it was still a predator to be reckoned with.
