Prehistoric Kingdom

A fast ostrich-like dinosaur built for running as suggested with its long legs, lightweight body, and toothless beak. While many other dinosaurs relied on defensive weapons such as horns, spikes, or armor, the Gallimimus used its speed to escape predators in open environments. Though if cornered it may have kicked with its legs in order to fight back if it must.
