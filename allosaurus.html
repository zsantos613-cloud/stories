The dominating predator of the Jurassic's Morrison Formation, the Allosaurus was an aggressive even for a carnivorous theropod as many of their fossils indicated that the animal led a combative lifestyle due to the many injuries shown in said fossils. Its belligerent nature led it to get into many encounters with its rival, the Stegosaurus. While not as large as Tyrannosaurus, the Allosaurus remains just as iconic as the 'Lion of the Jurassic'!
