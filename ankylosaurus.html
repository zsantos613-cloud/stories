Prehistoric Kingdom

An armored dinosaur with a massive club at the end of its tail. It's armor being made of thick bony plates known as a osteoderms making the dinosaur a 'living tank' and the ultimate defensive build against any predator. It's low-slung body size and leaf shaped teeth made it perfectly adapted to browsing low-lying plants.
