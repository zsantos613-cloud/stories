PERIOD

SOURCE

Path of Titans

In stegosaurian fashion the Miragaia also had a thagomizer and a set of plates that it used for defense. Differentiating itself from its more famous cousin Miragaia had an elongated body and an unusually long neck for a stegosaur. It also possessed bony plates making this wall of spikes a hard meal for any hungry predator.
