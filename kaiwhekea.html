SQUID EATER

PISCIVORE🐟

PERIOD

Late Cretaceous

FAMILY

Elasmoauridae

REGION

Oceania

SOURCE

Path of Titans

A relatively small plesiosaur found in New Zealand as it had a short neck compared to others in the same family. Like the meaning of its name suggests it likely fed on squid as well as fish. As a plesiosaur it had a four strong paddle-shaped flippers allowing it to move underwater and a long neck for catching prey.
