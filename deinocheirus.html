TERRIBLE HAND

OMNIVORE🐟🌿

PERIOD

FAMILY

Deinocheiridae

REGION

Asia

SOURCE

Prehistoric Kingdom

One of the largest ornithomimosaurs to ever live, the Deinocheirus is best known for its massive arms, in fact it may have had one of the largest arms in the animal kingdom. Possessing a duck-like beak and a bulky body and a semi-aquatic lifestyle feeding on both plants and fish, it is definitely one of the most unusual dinosaurs out there. Couple it with the fact that its comparable in size with Tarbosaurus and you have a surprisingly formidable animal!
