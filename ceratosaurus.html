A medium-sized carnivorous theropod recognizable by its distinctive nasal and brown horns alongside it's huge blade-like teeth. Sharing an ecosystem with other deadly animals within the Morrison Formation like Allosaurus whom it may have competited with! If the Allosaurus is the Lion of the Jurassic then the Ceratosaurus may have been the 'hyena'.
