HERBIVORE🌿

PERIOD

Early Jurassic

FAMILY

Dilophosauridae

REGION

North America

SOURCE

Prehistoric Kingdom

A slender medium sized theropod with a pair of beautfiul crests atop its head. Despite popular depictions from movies like Jurassic Park, the real life Dilophosaurus did not possess a neck frill or spit out deadly venom (though some could dream!). Being a lightweight predator it likely hunted its prey with grace, speed, and agility.
