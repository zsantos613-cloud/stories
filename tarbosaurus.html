While Tyrannosaurus was King of the Dinosaurs in the West, ruling the East was none other than the Alarming Lizard, Tarbosaurus! While it did not possess bone shattering jaws like its cousin, the Tarbosaurus was slimmer and lightly built with its jaws having a 'locking mechanicsm' allowing this Tyrant to rip the flesh off its prey.
