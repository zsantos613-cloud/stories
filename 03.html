Spinosaurus aegyptiacus

Spined Lizard

A peculiar amongst the megatheropod class. The Spinosaurus had a massive sail on its back, a like crocodilian snouth, and a paddle-like tail suggesting it's semi aquatic lifestyle. Unlike the Giganotosaurus or Tyrannosaurus, the Spinosaurus specialized in hunting fish and had a piscivorous diet spending time in freshwater hunting fish.
