Dinosaurs weren't the only reptiles that dominated the Mesozoic Era. So let's introduce the Sarcosuchus the 'super croc', a crocodyliform with an elongated snout. Like modern day crocodiles, the Sarcosuchus may have also ambushed large prey from the water, subduing them with its jaws an drowning them.
