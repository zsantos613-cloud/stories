Despite having 'raptor' in its name, the Megaraptors aren't dromaeosaurid dinosaurs but rather their own family. Lightly built with long arms adorned with deadly claws unlike other theropod groups make the megaraptors terrifying predators. The largest of the megaraptor theropods was none other than the Maip macrothorax whose name is derived from Aokenik mythology, for it's prey surely feared "the shadow of death".
