A recently described specimen and honoring the Norse God 'Loki', the Lokiceratops is yet another example of how diverse the ceratopsian dinosaur family can be! It's most distinct feature is its blade like horns that protrude from the back of its frill giving the dinosaur a menacing look, almost resembling its namesake.
