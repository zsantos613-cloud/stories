FAMILY

REGION

South America

SOURCE

Path of Titans

A distinctive sauropod known for its spines that run along its neck. It is currently debated whether or not that the spikes were used for defense or if they supported a sail that would help with thermoregulation. It may not have been the largest sauropod but it indeed stands out in terms of appearance.
