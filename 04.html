Prehistoric Kingdom

A member of the Hadrosauridae family known as "duck-billed" dinosaur the Edmontosaurus was a large plant eating dinosaur that lived in the Hell Creek Formation living alongide and potentially being preyed upon by Tyrannosaurus Rex. While not as armed with natural weapons or defense like its neighbors, the Edmontosaurus used its sheer sized and herd behavior to combat predators.
