Many who think of the dromaeosauridae family mainly think of the Velociraptor or Deinonychus, but none compare to the Utahraptor. While many of the 'raptor' dinosaurs are small relying on their cunning and speed to take down prey, the Utahraptor used its sheer strength to grapple enemies with the family's signature sickle-like talons!
